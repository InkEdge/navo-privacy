隐私政策

最后更新：2025年10月28日

Navo（以下简称“我们”）非常重视您的隐私。本隐私政策旨在说明您在使用我们的移动应用程序时，我们如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的信息。

本地优先

照片、视频及相关分析均在设备本地完成，不上传至服务器。

加密保护

隐私内容采用 AES-GCM 256 位加密存储，并结合 PBKDF2 强化密钥安全。

最小必要

仅在功能需要且经您授权时访问相关权限，不收集不必要的身份信息。

一、我们收集的信息

  • 照片与视频：仅在您授权的情况下访问，用于清理建议、导入与本地分析，所有操作均在设备本地完成。
  • 相机与麦克风：仅在您主动使用相关功能时调用，用于拍摄、录制并加密存储于隐私保险箱。
  • 设备信息：仅用于提升产品性能、兼容性和稳定性，不涉及个人身份信息。
  • 订阅信息：仅用于验证您的订阅状态及管理订阅功能。

二、我们如何使用您的信息

  • 实现、提供并维护应用的核心功能；
  • 提供智能清理建议与媒体整理能力；
  • 进行订阅管理与订阅状态验证；
  • 支持隐私保险箱的访问验证与安全保护；
  • 进行性能优化、兼容性提升与错误修复。

三、图像分析功能

  • 相似内容检测：使用 Vision 框架提取图像特征向量，以识别相似照片和视频。
  • 内容分类：自动识别 Live Photo、连拍照片、截图等媒体类型。
  • 本地处理：所有分析均在您的设备本地完成，不会上传至服务器。
  • 特征存储：为提升性能，应用会在设备本地存储图像特征向量。

说明：图像分析仅用于实现相似内容检测、媒体分类与性能优化，不用于广告、跟踪或画像分析。

四、权限请求

权限 用途 是否必需
照片库 用于清理建议、媒体导入与本地分析 必须
添加到照片库 用于保存文件或导出媒体 可选
相机 用于拍摄并保存隐私内容 可选

五、数据保留与安全

  • 本地存储：所有数据均保留在您的设备本地，绝不会上传到我们的服务器或第三方。
  • 加密算法：我们使用 AES-GCM 256 位加密算法保护存储数据。
  • 密钥管理：采用 PBKDF2 密钥派生机制，并使用 100,000 次迭代增强安全性。
  • 会话管理：在 5 分钟无操作后自动锁定，以保护隐私数据安全。
  • 完整性验证：使用 SHA256 哈希验证数据完整性。
  • 自动清理：系统会自动清理缓存、临时文件和过期日志。

六、数据保留政策

  • 本地存储：相关数据将持续保留在您的设备上，直至您主动删除。
  • 卸载应用：卸载应用后，保存在应用本地容器中的数据将被删除。
  • 特征数据：为提升性能生成的特征向量会保留在设备本地。
  • 缓存清理：应用会自动清理缓存和临时文件。

七、第三方服务

本应用不集成任何第三方分析、广告或跟踪服务。订阅相关能力仅依赖苹果官方提供的订阅服务。

八、您的权利

您可随时在系统设置中撤销相关权限，也可通过卸载 App 删除所有本地数据。如对本政策有任何疑问，请联系：chenyisy@gmail.com。

九、儿童隐私

我们不会有意收集 13 岁以下儿童的信息。

十、国际法规遵守

我们遵守包括 GDPR、CCPA 在内的适用法律法规，并尊重和保障您的相关权利。

十一、政策更新

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 28, 2025

Navo (“we”, “us”, or “our”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

On-device First

Your photos, videos, and related analysis stay on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

Encrypted Storage

Private content is protected with AES-GCM 256-bit encryption and strengthened with PBKDF2 key derivation.

Minimal Access

Permissions are requested only when needed for core features and only with your authorization.

1. Information We Collect

  • Photos & Videos: Accessed only with your permission for cleanup suggestions, import, and on-device analysis.
  • Camera & Microphone: Used only when you actively use capture features to record and encrypt private content in the vault.
  • Device Information: Used solely to improve performance, compatibility, and stability, without personal identifiers.
  • Subscription Information: Used only to verify subscription status and manage subscription-related features.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain core app functionality
  • To offer smart cleanup suggestions and media organization features
  • To manage subscriptions and verify subscription status
  • To support private vault authentication and security protection
  • To improve performance, compatibility, and fix errors

3. Image Analysis Features

  • Similarity Detection: Uses Apple’s Vision framework to extract feature vectors and identify similar photos and videos.
  • Content Classification: Automatically identifies media types such as Live Photos, burst photos, and screenshots.
  • On-device Processing: All analysis is performed locally on your device.
  • Feature Storage: Feature vectors may be stored locally on your device to improve performance.

Note: Image analysis is used only for similarity detection, media classification, and performance optimization. It is not used for advertising, tracking, or profiling.

4. Permissions Requested

Permission Purpose Required
Photo Library Cleanup suggestions, import, and on-device analysis Yes
Add to Photo Library Save files or export media Optional
Camera Capture and store private content Optional
Microphone Audio recording during video capture Optional

5. Data Retention & Security

  • Local Storage: All data remains on your device and is never uploaded to our servers or any third party.
  • Encryption: AES-GCM 256-bit encryption is used to protect stored data.
  • Key Management: PBKDF2 key derivation with 100,000 iterations is used to enhance security.
  • Session Management: The app automatically locks after 5 minutes of inactivity.
  • Integrity Verification: SHA-256 hashing is used to verify data integrity.
  • Automatic Cleanup: Cache, temporary files, and expired logs are automatically removed.

6. Data Retention Policy

  • On-device Storage: Data remains on your device until you delete it.
  • App Uninstall: Uninstalling the app removes data stored in the app’s local container.
  • Feature Data: Feature vectors generated for performance may remain locally on your device.
  • Cache Cleanup: Temporary files and cache are cleaned automatically.

7. Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Subscription-related features rely only on Apple’s official subscription services.

8. Your Rights

You may revoke permissions at any time in your system settings, or delete the app to remove local data. If you have any questions, please contact: chenyisy@gmail.com.

9. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

10. International Compliance

We comply with applicable laws and regulations, including GDPR and CCPA, and respect your related rights.

11. Policy Updates

If this Privacy Policy is updated, we will notify you within the app.
